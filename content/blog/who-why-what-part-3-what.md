So what do I actually do?

Over the last decade, I’ve worked across a wide range of creative development and animation projects — from interactive websites to animated banners, from data visualizations to 3D experiments. My work always circles back to one core passion: bringing ideas to life through motion and interactivity.

**Core Focus: Web Animation & Interactive Experiences**

I specialize in:

- 🎨 GSAP-powered animations — scroll storytelling, parallax, smooth timelines, and ScrollTrigger effects
- ✒️ SVG animations — vector morphs, line draws, icons, and detailed logo reveals
- 🌀 Canvas & Three.js projects — interactive 2D/3D elements and immersive visuals
- 📢 Google Ads & HTML5 banners — lightweight, high-performance animations for campaigns
- 📜 Scroll-based storytelling — narrative-driven sites where motion guides the user experience

Tweaking timing, easing, and transitions until everything feels silky smooth is my jam. For me, animations are not just decoration — they’re storytelling tools. As an avid reader, I treat motion the same way I treat a good story: it needs flow, rhythm, and a vibe that pulls you in.

**Beyond Animation: Full-Stack Creative Development**

Animations rarely live on their own — they’re part of bigger projects. Over the years I’ve delivered:

- 🌍 Web applications & landing pages (Nuxt, Vue, WordPress, headless builds)
- 🛒 E-commerce sites (WooCommerce, custom checkout flows, headless carts)
- 📐 Responsive design systems (CSS Grid, Flexbox, utility classes)
- 🎨 Design conversions (Figma, PSD, XD → pixel-perfect code)
- ⚡ AI workflow integration (prompt design, automation pipelines)

Frameworks and tools I work with daily include: Nuxt.js, Vue.js, GSAP, Three.js, WordPress, SCSS/Sass, JavaScript/ES6+, Tailwind CSS, PHP, and Git. For bigger builds, I’m comfortable with Webpack, Docker, and TypeScript.

**Portfolio Scope**

My projects span industries and formats — some highlights include:

- 🚀 Interactive agency work with Green Rubino, We Push Buttons, We Are Original People, Barenholt
- 📢 Large HTML5 banner suites and Google Ads campaigns
- 🎶 Creative media — music videos, animated logos, and glitch art pieces
- 📊 Data visualization tools and interactive dashboards
- 🛠 Specialized apps — from blockchain experiments to p2p local-first apps

Whether it’s a simple animated icon or a fully custom-built web platform, the goal is always the same: create something that feels alive, engaging, and meaningful.
