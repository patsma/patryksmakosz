Hi, I’m Patryk.

I wear many hats — dreamer, reader, writer, stoic, task-executioner, animator, developer, “computers guy,” music guy, graphics guy, drone pilot, occasional archer, and video editor… probably a few more if I think long enough. 😉

When I’m not building or animating something on the web, you’ll likely find me:

- Walking or cycling in nature with my better half
- Kayaking with friends
- Practicing bushcraft or enjoying the forest
- Hanging out with animals (currently: four sweet chinchillas 🐹🐹🐹🐹)

**Early Days**

I was born in 1990 in the coastal city of Gdynia, Poland, though most of my life I spent in the countryside.

My story with computers started around age 10–12. They felt like magic, the closest thing humans had to bending reality. Around that time I also fell in love with books — especially fantasy and sci-fi (Ender’s Game, Lord of the Rings, and any story that could transport me elsewhere).

I followed the “official path” with Computer Science through to college, but eventually found the theory outdated and uninspiring. So I turned to self-learning, trying everything from design and animation to completely non-digital jobs — a way to balance out all those hours in front of screens.

**Why “Tasty Sites”?**

My last name, Smakosz, translates roughly to “the Taster.” That’s the origin of Tasty Sites:

- Websites → creative, handcrafted digital experiences
- Tasty sites of life → adventures, drone clips, and moments I share on my [YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/)

**Beyond the Nerdy Stuff**

Despite the tech-heavy CV, I’m a social creature at heart. People, friendships, and connections are what keep me grounded. I love forest walks, riding bikes, gaming nights with friends, and discovering new stories in every form — books, games, films, or conversations.
